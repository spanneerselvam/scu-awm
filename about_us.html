Our Mission

Our goal is to mentor and encourage young women and other students at SCU as they prepare for careers in the mathematical sciences and STEM fields. We want to empower women to pursue their dreams and passion for mathematics so that they are unstoppable.

Our Story

AWM's chapter at SCU was originally started by the late Professor Jean Pederson, who's love for math and her students was illustrated through her teaching and mentorship of AWM. AWM used to only have one event--which is now known as our "Math Murder Mystery Night"--and only three members... Two years later, over 60 students have enrolled in AWM and are participating in our exciting events! AWM continues to foster support towards their members as they pursue careers in the STEM field.
